About Me

I am an FPGA Engineer specializing in hardware acceleration for AI and high-performance digital systems. My work focuses on designing efficient RTL architectures and deploying lightweight CNN models on FPGA platforms such as Zynq UltraScale+.

I have hands-on experience in Verilog/SystemVerilog, AXI-based system design, DMA optimization, and hardware–software co-design. I have developed reconfigurable CNN accelerators, implemented MobileNetV2 inference on FPGA, and worked on RISC-V-based hardware systems for real-time applications.

In addition, I have a strong background in UVM-based functional verification, including constrained-random verification environments and SystemVerilog Assertions (SVA).

I am particularly interested in edge AI, autonomous systems, and building scalable hardware accelerators for next-generation intelligent devices.
